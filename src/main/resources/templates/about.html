About

G4Tec Engineering are proud to be one of the leading civil contractors in the Cambodia resources and infrastructure sectors. The strong success of G4Tec's Civil Contractor Division has been built on a foundation of consistently delivering project works to the highest standards. Since beginning as a small civil contractor, G4Tec has demonstrated an outstanding safety record and the capacity to deliver high quality projects on time and on budget.

STRATEGY

• Our strategic objective is to deliver projects that fulfill the customer’s specifications. These specifications include the project being delivered on time, within budget and complete legitimacy.

• Our leverage is derived from a wide spectrum of social groups and our expertise is to deliver medium and large projects in South Africa and Southern Africa.

• In order to fulfill our strategic objective, we apply a unique combination of design, construction and project management.

Our Mission

Our Vision
